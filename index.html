BUILT FOR PARENTS TO TRUST

Safe by design

  • 🚫
    No ads, ever

    Nothing to click away, nothing to buy. TinyTaps will always be free and ad-free.

  • 🔒
    No accounts, no tracking

    We never collect your child's data. The only number we know is how many devices are playing right now — anonymously.

  • ✈️
    Works offline

    Add it to your home screen once and it works on flights, in cars, anywhere — no internet needed.

  • 🔐
    Toddler-locked

    No visible buttons for little fingers. Parents hold the top-left corner for 2 seconds to open the menu.

  • 🎵
    Gentle by design

    Pentatonic sounds (everything sounds musical), soft colours, and a voice that celebrates your child by name.
